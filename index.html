Japan

Fun Fact: Japan is an archipelago, or string of islands, on the eastern edge of Asia. There are four main islands: Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, and Kyushu. There are also nearly 4,000 smaller islands!

Greece

Fun Fact: Greece has the longest coastline in Europe and is the southernmost country in Europe.

Spain

Fun Fact: Spain occupies most of the Iberian Peninsula, stretching south from the Pyrenees Mountains to the Strait of Gibraltar, which separates Spain from Africa.

Australia

Fun Fact: Australia is the only country in the world that covers an entire continent. It is one of the largest countries on Earth.
