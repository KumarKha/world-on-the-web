Japan

Where to Visit

Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower is a communications and observation tower in the Shiba-Koen district Tokyo, Japan. It is the second tallest tower in Japan standing at 634 meters

Golden Pavilion

The Golden Pavilion is a zen temple, formally know as the retirement villa of the shogun Ashikaga Yoshimitsu. It was burnt down a number of time and has been rebuilt in 1955

Mount. Fuji

Mount Fuji is one the most iconic symbols in Japan. Standing at 3,776 meter the mountain is an active volcano which many flock to mountain for inspiration.
